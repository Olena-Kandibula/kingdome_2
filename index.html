Development prospects

According to research by specialists from Glampitect home, glamping isn’t a new phenomenon, but its popularity has skyrocketed in recent years. Industry reports show that the global glamping industry is set to reach $4.1 billion by 2028, expanding at a CAGR of 14.1%, and the ROI from glamping sites regularly outstrips the returns from more conventional investment. Booking.com has almost 1,000 glamping locations around the world, and the cost of overnight stays varies from $50 to $2-3 thousand. Judging by surveys, luxury camping - glamping - is in greatest demand. A high price is the price to pay for the opportunity to be in a good place with maximum contact with nature, often with wild nature.
